Our Ministries

Find your place to serve, grow, and make a difference

At Laver Transformation Ministries, we believe everyone has a calling and a purpose. Our ministries provide opportunities for you to use your gifts, grow in faith, and serve others. Whether you're a youth, a parent, a senior, or anywhere in between, there's a place for you here.

Our Youth Ministry is dedicated to raising up young people who are rooted in faith and ready to impact their generation. Through worship, fellowship, and discipleship, we help youth discover their God-given potential.

Our Beliefs

We practice believer's baptism by immersion as a public declaration of faith in Jesus Christ. Baptism services are held quarterly.

We have various ministry opportunities available. After attending our membership class, you can join our ministry teams based on your gifts and interests.

We primarily use the New International Version (NIV) in our services, but we welcome all translations.

Our History & Vision

The journey of faith that brought us here and the path that lies ahead

2010 - Our Humble Beginnings

Starting as a small prayer group in a living room, our founding members came together with a shared vision of creating a welcoming spiritual community in Wamala.

2015 - Growing Community

As our congregation grew, we moved to our first dedicated building and established our core ministries, focusing on youth development and community outreach.

2020 - New Sanctuary

God blessed us with the opportunity to build our current sanctuary, providing more space for worship and ministry activities.

2024 - Present Day

Today, we continue to grow and serve, reaching out to our community through various programs and ministries.

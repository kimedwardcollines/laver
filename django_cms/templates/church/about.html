Our Story

{% if church and church.history %}

{{ church.history|safe }}

{% else %}

Laver Transformation Ministries was established with a clear vision: to be a beacon of hope and transformation in the Wamala community. What started as a small gathering of believers has grown into a vibrant community dedicated to spreading God's love and transforming lives through the power of His Word.

Located along Wamala Katoke Road, our church has become a spiritual home for families, youth, and individuals seeking a deeper relationship with God. We believe in the power of community, worship, and service to bring about lasting transformation in people's lives.

What We Believe

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and the ultimate authority for faith and practice.

Trinity

We believe in one God, existing in three persons: Father, Son (Jesus Christ), and Holy Spirit.

Salvation

We believe salvation comes through faith in Jesus Christ, who died for our sins and rose again.

Transformation

We believe God is actively transforming lives today through the Holy Spirit's power.

The Church

We believe the church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, and serve together.

Eternity

We believe in eternal life for believers and the return of Jesus Christ to establish His kingdom.
